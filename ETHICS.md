# Ethical AI Framework

## Our Commitment

The Urban Evolution AI Platform is committed to responsible and ethical AI development. This framework outlines our principles and practices.

## Core Principles

### 1. Transparency
- All AI decisions are explainable
- Model limitations are clearly communicated
- Data sources are documented

### 2. Fairness
- Bias detection in all predictions
- Equal consideration of all demographic groups
- Regular audits for discriminatory patterns

### 3. Accountability
- Human oversight for major decisions
- Clear responsibility chains
- Audit logs for all operations

### 4. Privacy
- Minimal data collection
- Secure data handling
- Compliance with privacy regulations

### 5. Environmental Responsibility
- Carbon footprint tracking
- Sustainable urban development focus
- Resource efficiency optimization

## Implementation

### Bias Detection
- EthicsGuardian agent reviews all scenarios
- Automated checks for:
  - Displacement risk
  - Environmental impact
  - Equity and fairness
  - Cultural preservation

### Human-in-the-Loop
- Required for ethics scores below 0.7
- Required for high-cost operations
- User approval for all transformations

### Continuous Monitoring
- Regular bias audits
- Community feedback integration
- Transparent reporting

## Reporting Concerns
